About

Founded in 2013 by Thomas Deneuville, Sustainist Media publishes award-winning digital content.

Our first and main publication is I CARE IF YOU LISTEN, a magazine + a blog that aims to provide accessible information and opinion on contemporary concert music (as well as art and technology).

In the spring of 2013, we partnered with TypeEngine, a Seattle-based startup, to launch a magazine available in the Apple Newsstand (iOS devices) and Google Play (Android devices).

Read an interview with Sustainist Media’s Thomas Deneuville on TypeEngine’s blog, or listen to an episode of POP TECH JAM to learn more about this partnership.

In October 2013, the ASCAP Foundation Deems Taylor Media Award recognized I CARE IF YOU LISTEN and its founder and editor, Thomas Deneuville.

In October 2014, I CARE IF YOU LISTEN was a finalist at the Digital Music Awards, in the Music Magazine of the Year category.

Our name was inspired by the work of Michiel Schwarz and Joost Elffers.
